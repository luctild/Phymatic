ABOUT-US

PHYMATICS CHAMPIONS CLUB

Phymatics Champions club is a club that aims to promote physics and mathematics among students.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn and share knowledge of physics and mathematics.

Our mission is to become a leading club in promoting physics and mathematics among students.

Phymatics Champions is found at Buikwe Senior Secondary with is located in Najjembe sub-county.

This club was built-up by Pro.Bazira Dathan and now, he is the patron of the club.
